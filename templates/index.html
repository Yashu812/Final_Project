Virtual Try On


Virtual Try On Project helps consumers to try the clothes virtually without physically buying them. The consumers can virtually try the desired cloth on their body by the mode of this website. The consumer will upload the picture his/her along with the desired cloth which he/she wants to try-on on this website. Then, the pictures will be sent to our model for processing the pictures, segmenting the key body points, wrapping the cloth, fusing the content and generating the final output. This output image with desired cloth wrapped on the consumer's body will be dispalyed on the website.
